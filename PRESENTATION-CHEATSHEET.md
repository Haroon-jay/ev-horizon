# EV Horizon — Presentation Cheat Sheet

**Setup:** refresh home tab right before (hero numbers animate live) · pre-open tabs: home → a tool → an article → search → contact · have phone ready (responsive).

---

## SAY + SHOW (≈5 min)

**1 · HOOK** *(home page)*
> "I'm an EV enthusiast. Every answer I wanted — *real savings? charging time? is this battery healthy?* — was scattered across ten sites, half hype, half jargon. So I built the place I wished existed. I built it for myself, and for everyone Googling the same questions."

**2 · POSITIONING** *(About)*
> "One rule: most EV coverage is either hype or jargon. EV Horizon sits in between — honest, clear, for everyone. **That's my core marketing message.**"

**3 · TOOLS ⭐** *(open /tools/savings/, type real numbers, calculate live)*
> "What makes it more than a blog: 5 free calculators. Savings, charging cost, charging time, range, battery health. Free tools are the best **traffic magnet** there is — this is my customer-acquisition engine."

**4 · CONTENT** *(Tesla vs BYD article — scroll headings, FAQ, photo)*
> "10 in-depth articles — real photos, FAQs, related links. Structured the way people actually read, and good for Google."

**5 · NAV + SEARCH** *(click nav, then /search/ → type "Tesla")*
> "Sticky menu, categories, breadcrumbs — and a real **search**: by brand, model, topic, or tool."

**6 · CONTACT** *(/contact/)*
> "Visitors reach me easily — contact form, socials, and **'enter your email'** newsletter on every page to build an audience."

**7 · ADS ⭐** *(scroll home → "Advertising Opportunities")*
> "Monetizable from day one — example placements from charging networks, insurers, solar. Clearly labelled 'Ad' / 'Sponsored' to keep editorial trust."

**8 · BUILT TO SCALE ⭐** *(closer)*
> "I didn't use a template — I built a system that generates the whole site. Write one article and every page, the menu, search and sitemap update. Every story is also built to become a video, TikTok and newsletter. **Built to scale, not just to submit.**"

**CLOSE**
> "One clear, trustworthy home for everything electric — useful enough that people return, built so I can grow it into a real publication. Thank you."

## PRINCIPLES SCORECARD (name these)

| Principle | Proof |
|---|---|
| Text / message | "Hype vs jargon — clear for everyone" |
| Visuals | Animated hero + real photos |
| Navigation | Sticky nav, categories, breadcrumbs |
| Search | `/search/` by brand/model/topic/tool |
| Interactivity | 5 live calculators, drag-to-charge hero |
| Contact / email | Form + newsletter every page |
| Content variety | Articles · videos · tools · infographics |
| Right amount | 10 focused, substantial articles |
| Advertising | Labelled, industry-relevant placements |

## Q&A QUICK ANSWERS

- **AI built it?** → "AI for research only; design, build system, tool logic and voice are mine — not a template."
- **Actually online?** → "Static site — deploys to free hosting (Netlify) with a domain in minutes." *(Deploy first if you can.)*
- **Forms work?** → "Contact opens a pre-filled email; newsletter is a demo that connects to a real provider in one step."
- **Make money?** → "Display ads + sponsored content from EV brands, plus affiliate links on buying guides — high-intent buyers."
- **Photos?** → "Free-license — Unsplash + manufacturer press kits, credited."

**Emphasize 3:** ① the personal *why* ② the **tools** (demo live) ③ **built to scale.**
