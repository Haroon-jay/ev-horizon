Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

⚡

Two-line embed

A div and a script tag. No npm, no build step, no developer required. Works on any website including WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, and custom HTML.

🎨

Brand-matched styling

Set your brand color with one attribute. The widget auto-adapts — no CSS overrides needed. Your logo or dealer name appears in the header.

📬

Automatic lead capture

After a customer sees their savings, a "Book a Test Drive" form appears. Leads are posted to your webhook (Zapier, Make, your CRM) with full calc context.

🌍

km & miles, any currency

Customers toggle between metric and imperial. All inputs use real-world defaults that make sense — no confusion, no wrong answers.

📱

Fully responsive

Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop without any configuration. Single-column layout on small screens automatically.

🔒

Privacy-friendly

No cookies, no tracking pixels, no third-party scripts. Calculations run entirely in the browser — nothing is sent to our servers unless a lead submits.

How it works

Up and running in 5 minutes

1

Sign up

Create your account and get your API key and embed code instantly.

2

Paste two lines

Add the div and script tag to any page on your site. Set your brand color, dealer name, and CTA text via attributes.

3

Connect your CRM

Paste your Zapier or Make webhook URL. Every lead arrives with name, email, phone, and the savings numbers they calculated.

4

Get leads

Customers who've already calculated their savings are warm leads — they know the numbers and they're curious. You just show up for the test drive.

Your embed code (customise the attributes):

<div id="ev-savings-widget"></div> <script src="https://evhorizon.media/widget.js" data-dealer="Your Dealership Name" data-color="#00D4FF" data-unit="mi" data-model="Model 3" data-webhook="https://hooks.zapier.com/YOUR_HOOK" data-cta="Book a Test Drive" defer> </script>
