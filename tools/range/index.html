How it works

Base range = battery capacity ÷ consumption. We then apply real-world multipliers for temperature and driving style drawn from published fleet data.

Why winter range drops

Cold batteries are less efficient and cabin heating draws power. Heat pumps and preconditioning recover much of the loss — see Common EV Myths.
