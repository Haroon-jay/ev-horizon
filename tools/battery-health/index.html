    How it works

    We start from the fleet-average degradation rate (~1.8% per year) and adjust for the factors that accelerate or slow it: mileage, fast-charging share, 100% charging habits, climate and observed symptoms.

    Why LFP is different

    LFP batteries (most BYDs, standard-range Teslas) tolerate daily full charges far better than NMC packs — that’s why the answer options treat them differently.
